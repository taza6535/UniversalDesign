Lab 3 - Accessible Tables, Forms, and Tabbed Browsing

For this lab, we learned more accessibility techniques. We learned how to make elements like tables, forms, quotes, and highlighted text. My page for this lab was about Taylor Swift's The Eras Tour. I implemented an article navigation at the top of the page, utilizing Google's Material Icons. I implemented a list of the eras that are perfromed during the concert and used highlighting to mark the eras that are my favorite. I also have a table for all of the tour dates that were remaining for 2023 in Latin America. Finally, I implemented an accessible form for someone who may want to buy tickets for the tour.

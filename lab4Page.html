Lab 4 - CSS Grid/Flexbox

For this lab, we learned how to implement CSS Grid/Flexbox to make our sites more responsive. Grid/Flexbox allows you to format the layout of the page based on the screen size to optimize it for different devices such as laptops, tablets, or phones. I decided to do this page about Taylor Swift's latest album that came out in October: 1989 (Taylor's Version). I have a title at the top and a navbar for different sections on the page. On the left side is an aside with the full tracklist of the album, including From the Vault songs. The middle section is the main section where it gives information about the album. The right side is another aside that gives more information about what Taylor's Version is.
